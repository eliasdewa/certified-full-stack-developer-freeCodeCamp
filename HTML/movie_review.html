Movie Review: Green Book

Review: "Green Book" is a heartwarming and inspiring film that tells the true story of an unlikely friendship between a working-class Italian-American bouncer and an African-American classical pianist during the 1960s. The performances by Viggo Mortensen and Mahershala Ali are outstanding, bringing depth and authenticity to their characters. The film beautifully explores themes of racism, acceptance, and the power of music to bridge divides. With its engaging storytelling and emotional resonance, "Green Book" is a must-watch for anyone who appreciates a well-crafted narrative.

Movie Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ (8.8/10)

Director: Peter Farrelly

Release Year: 2018

Genre: Drama, Comedy

Cast Members:

  • Viggo Mortensen as Tony Lip
  • Mahershala Ali as Dr. Don Shirley
  • Linda Cardellini as Dolores
